About Air Fryer 2-Ingredient Mozzarella Sticks

These air-fried 2-ingredient mozzarella sticks are gooey bites of delicious string cheese, wrapped in a tortilla, and cooked until golden in an air fryer. This is a quick snack for after school, a quick lunch, or for any moment when you need something easy and satisfying.

Ingredients You'll Need for Mozzarella Sticks

  • 8 sticks string cheese
  • 8 (6 inch) flour tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely crushed dried parsley flakes
  • marinara sauce, for dipping (optional)

Steps

  1. Place a cheese stick at the bottom third of each tortilla. Tuck the sides in and roll up. Use a toothpick to secure the tortilla closed if necessary.
  2. Brush each roll with olive oil and sprinkle lightly with garlic powder and dried parsley.
  3. Fry in an air fryer at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) until just browned and toasted, about 5 minutes.
  4. Serve with marinara if desired.
